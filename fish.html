Sichuan Style Grilled Fish

Ingredients:

  • 1 fish
  • 1/2 ginger root, 5 green onions, 1 whole garlic
  • 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp cumin powder, 2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 10 dry red pepper, 0.1oz Sichuan pepper corn
  • 1 onion, Sorted vegetables
  • 1 tbsp cilantro
  • 1 package of Wangjiadu spicy stir-fry sauce
  • vegetable oil

Cooking Directions:

  1. Marinate fish
    • Mix marinate sauce: In a mixing bowl, add 5 cloves minced garlic, chopped ginger root, salt, pepper, cumin, cayenne pepper, and some Chinese cooking wine.
    • Clean the fish. Make several cuts on both sides of the fish. Rub in with mixed marinate sauce, set aside marinating for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Bake fish
    • Pre-heat oven to 425F.
    • Place 4 sliced(about 2 inches) green onion and about 8 thin slices of ginger root evenly on a baking pan.
    • Bake the marinated fish for 15 minutes on each side.
  3. Clean and slice your choice of vegetables and julienne onion. Sautee with Wangjiadu spicy stir-fry sauce and remaining spicies. Add some about 1 cup of water, wait until boiling.
  4. Transfer everything from cooking pan to baking pan.
  5. Turn oven to broil, broil for another 5 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle chopped cilantro on top and serve.
