Green Onion Noodle

Ingredients:

	Egg noodle
	1 green onions, 1 tsp red pepper flakes
	1/2 tbsp light soy sauce, 1/2 tsp granulated sugar, 1/2 tsp chicken essence
	1 tsp vegetable oil


Cooking Directions:

	1. Dice green onion.
	2. Cook noodle for about 5 minutes or until soften. Transfer to a bowl.
	3. Put soy sauce, sugar, and chicken essence into the bowl, mix well.
	4. Place red pepper flakes and chopped green onion on top of the noodle.
	5. Heat up oil, when it's smoking hot, pour it on top of noodle bowl.
	6. Mix and enjoy.
